Background

I research the market and test product features to make sure that our products are easy to use and are competitive against other products on the market. My background in studying human centered design really helped me understand and view the product from the user’s standpoint.

ARI Catalogue

I was tasked with creating a catalogue for CES 2019. It was my first time using Adobe InDesign and making a catalogue so I just learned as I go.

First I had to organize our product information into a understandable heirarchy. The products were separated by wireless chargers and dash cams, and within each categories I further grouped them into sub categories. To understand each product I had to contact product engineers and determine what specifications and information to display for each product. Overall I learned how to utilize InDesign to create a product catalogue and how to organize product into clear hierarchy.
